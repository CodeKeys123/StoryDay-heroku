What is StoryDay?

I know, You might have known about StoryDay before entering here. If you already know about this site then you can skip this question. For those who don't know and want to know here is the explanation. StoryDay is a website where you can read stories and novels for free. FOr uploading story you have to contact us through the E-Mail which is present at the end of our homepage. In the future people can only upload 3 stories or 2 novels per 3 months for free. If they want to upload more stories then they have to choose one of our donation plan. But always, reading story is free. You can read as many stories as you want.

how many stories are there in StoryDay?

To be honest, even we do not know how many stories are there. We try to upload atleast 5 stories every months. If any one of your favourite story is missing then free feel to mail us. We will try to add it without any copyright issue.
